About StresSense

StresSense is an AI-powered stress management application designed to help users monitor and manage their stress levels. By analyzing real-time physiological and behavioral data, such as heart rate and voice patterns, StresSense provides personalized feedback and recommendations aimed at improving overall well-being.

How It Works

Our system collects data from smartwatches and other sensors, utilizing APIs like Apple HealthKit to monitor physiological metrics like heart rate variability (HRV). Our Goal

To offer real-time, science-backed insights into your stress levels, empowering you to take proactive steps in stress management. Whether you are an individual seeking better wellness or a healthcare provider looking for stress monitoring tools, StresSense offers actionable feedback and easy-to-understand notifications to guide your stress management journey.
